What you need to know

What Are Spell Casting Services?

Spell casting services are offered by spellcasters who use ritual, energy work, folk magic, or spiritual traditions to influence outcomes in a person's life. This can include love spells, money spells, protection rituals, hex removal, court case spells, and much more.

Traditions vary widely. A Wiccan witch may use candle magic and lunar cycles. A hoodoo rootworker may use herbs, oils, and conjure bags. A voodoo priest may work with spirits and ritual offerings. A psychic medium may channel guidance from the other side. Despite the differences in method, all of these fall under the broad category of spell casting services.

The best online spell casters are transparent about their methods, honest about what is realistically possible, and supported by reviews from clients who have actually worked with them. That's exactly what this directory helps you find.

What sets legit spell casters apart

Signs of a Legit Spell Caster

  • Multiple verified reviews from real paying clients
  • Clear, upfront pricing with no hidden fees
  • Honest about limitations - no guaranteed outcomes
  • Responds clearly before, during, and after work
  • Does not use fear or urgency to pressure you
  • Explains their tradition and methods openly
  • Doesn't keep asking for more money
  • Has a consistent online presence over time
Common questions

Spell Casters Directory - FAQs

How do I find a legit online spell caster?
Look for spellcasters with multiple detailed reviews from verified clients. On Witch Weekly, every reviewer signs in with Google before posting, so reviews are tied to real accounts. Read what services were purchased, how the spellcaster communicated, and whether the reviewer would hire them again. Avoid anyone who guarantees results or demands large sums upfront.
What types of spell casters are there?
There are many traditions. Western Witchcraft includes Wiccans, hedge witches, and green witches. Folk Magic traditions include hoodoo rootworkers and conjure workers. African Diaspora traditions include voodoo and vodou priests. Latin/Caribbean traditions include brujos and curanderas. Other types include psychic mediums, tarot readers, astrologers, energy healers, shamans, and ceremonial magicians.
Are online spell casters real?
Yes, genuine online spell casters exist across many traditions. However, the online space also has many fraudulent spellcasters. The best way to tell them apart is through community reviews from people who have actually paid for and received their services - which is exactly what Witch Weekly provides.
How much do spell casting services cost?
Prices vary widely. Simple readings or candle work can cost $30 - $100. More complex spellwork like multi-step rituals or reconciliation work can range from $100 to $500+. Be cautious of very high prices tied to vague "curse removals" - this is a common scam tactic. Check each spellcaster's profile for pricing details.
What is the difference between a spell caster and a psychic?
A spell caster actively performs ritual work or energy manipulation to create a desired outcome. A psychic focuses on receiving and interpreting information about a person's situation. Many spellcasters offer both services. Some specialize only in readings (divination), while others offer only spellcasting or both.
