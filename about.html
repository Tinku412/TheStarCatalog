About Us

Built by the Community,
For the Community

Witch Weekly is a community-driven review platform where seekers can discover spell casters… and share honest experiences with others.

What We Are

A Review Platform, Plain and Simple

Witch Weekly is a community review directory for spell casters, psychics, energy healers, tarot readers, and all kinds of spiritual spellcasters. Think of it as a Yelp for the esoteric world — a place where real people share real experiences.

For Seekers
Browse the directory, read reviews, and make your own decision. No account required to explore listings.
For spellcasters
Any spellcaster can be listed here at no cost. No application, no gatekeeping. Anyone can submit a profile — including the spellcaster themselves.
Reviews
Anyone with a Google account can leave a review. All honest reviews — positive or negative — are welcome and will not be removed on a spellcaster's request.
Moderation
We do not moderate review content unless it contains illegal material, hate speech, or is clearly spam. The community self-regulates through upvotes and honest reviews.
Our Stance

Neutral. Unbiased. Open.

We take no position on whether any spellcaster is genuine, effective, or trustworthy. That is for the community to decide through reviews and upvotes.

Every review, every upvote, every submission is from a real community member. We are not affiliated with any spellcaster or spiritual organisation.

"Witch Weekly does not endorse any spellcaster, guarantee any results, or mediate any transactions. We are simply a platform for community voices."

Why We Built This

The Esoteric Space Needed Honest Reviews

Finding a trustworthy spell caster or spiritual spellcaster online is genuinely difficult. Most information comes from paid advertisements, fake testimonials, or communities with financial interests. There was no neutral, independent space for honest community reviews.

We built Witch Weekly to fill that gap. A simple, community-owned space where people can share what actually happened when they worked with a spellcaster — the good, the bad, and everything in between.
