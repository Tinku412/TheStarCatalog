My story — why I built this

I got scammed. More than once.

I was going through a really hard time. I wanted help — the kind of help you can't always find at a doctor or a therapist. So I started looking for spell casters. And I got taken for a ride.

  • I paid hundreds of dollars to people who vanished the moment the money hit
  • I found "reviews" on their pages that were clearly made up
  • I had no way to check if anyone else had a bad experience with them
  • There was nowhere to warn others — so the same people kept scamming

That's when I said enough. I built this so we could all share what we know — the good, the bad, and the ugly. Together, we can stop the fakes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions I get asked a lot

Is this actually free? What's the catch?
It's free for now. There are no fees to browse and no fees to get listed. I'm not selling anything. I built this out of personal frustration, not to make a business. Browsing and reviewing will always be free.
Can anyone submit a practitioner?
Yes — you don't have to be the practitioner to submit them. If you've worked with someone you trust, go ahead and add them. Same if you've worked with a scammer and want to warn others. The more people share, the better this works for everyone.
Are any practitioners paying to be listed or to rank higher?
No. Not a single one. I don't accept money for listings or rankings. Everyone in this directory got here because the community put them here. Order is based on reviews and upvotes — nothing else.
How do I know the reviews are real?
Reviewers have to sign in with Google before they can leave a review. That stops a lot of the fake stuff. We also ask what services they actually purchased, so there's a real transaction behind every review.
Are you guaranteeing anything about these practitioners?
No — and I want to be really clear about that. I'm just a directory. I share what the community says. I don't endorse anyone here. Please use your own judgment, do your research, and never send large amounts of money upfront to anyone.
I want to update or remove my profile. How do I do that?
Practitioner-controlled profiles are something I'm working on. For now, just reach out through the contact page and I'll take care of it for you quickly.
I found a scammer listed here. What now?
Please leave a review and describe exactly what happened. Then use the contact page to flag it. I take this seriously — reports from the community are the only way to keep this place clean.
What kinds of practitioners can I find here?
Spell casters, witches (of all kinds), psychics, tarot readers, astrologers, energy healers, shamans, mediums, rootworkers, voodoo practitioners, spiritual coaches, and a lot more. All traditions, all styles.
