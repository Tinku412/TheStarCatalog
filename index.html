Frequently Asked Questions

Spell Caster Directory — Frequently Asked Questions

Can anyone submit a spell caster to the directory?
Yes you don't have to be the spellcaster to submit them. If you've worked with someone you trust, go ahead and add them. Same if you've worked with a scammer and want to warn others. The more people share, the better this works for everyone.
Are any spell casters paying for better reviews or rankings?
No. Not a single one. We don't accept money for listings or rankings. Everyone in this directory got here because the community put them here.
How do I know the spell caster reviews are real?
Reviewers must sign in with Google before they can leave a review. We also encourage sharing proof of services purchased, so there's a real transaction behind every review.
Do you guarantee results from the spell casters listed?
No — and we want to be really clear about that. We're just a directory. We share what the community says. We don't endorse anyone here. Please use your own judgment, do your research, and never send large amounts of money upfront to anyone.
I want to update or remove my spell caster profile. How?
spellcaster-controlled profiles are something we're working on. For now, just reach out through the contact page and we'll take care of it for you quickly.
I found a scammer listed here. What now?
Please leave a review and describe exactly what happened along with any proof you have. Then use the contact page to flag it. We take this seriously — reports from the community are the only way to keep this place clean.
What kinds of spell casters and spellcasters can I find here?
Spell casters, witches, psychics, tarot readers, astrologers, energy healers, shamans, mediums, rootworkers, voodoo spellcasters, hoodoo conjure workers, spiritual coaches, and more. All traditions and styles are represented.

About this platform

The Spell Caster Directory Built by Seekers, for Seekers

Witch Weekly is a community-driven spell caster directory where anyone can read and leave honest reviews on witches, psychics, tarot readers, rootworkers, voodoo priests, energy healers, and every other kind of spiritual spellcaster. We were built out of personal experience — too many of us lost money to online scammers hiding behind fake reviews and paid testimonials.

When you search for trusted spell casters or spell caster reviews online, most results are either paid advertisements or platforms with no accountability. We are different. Every review on Witch Weekly comes from a real person who signed in with their Google account.

Our spell caster reviews cover key areas like communication, accuracy of results, value for money, and whether the reviewer would hire again so you can make a genuinely informed decision before reaching out to any spellcaster. You can also filter the directory by spell casting services, specialty, spellcaster type, and price range.

Whether you are looking for a love spell caster, a money and prosperity specialist, a hex removal expert, a court case spell caster, or a gifted tarot reader this is the place to start. Browse our full spell caster directory, read real reviews, and find a spellcaster you can actually trust.
